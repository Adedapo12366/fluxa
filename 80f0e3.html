Why It Matters

The Real Cost of Poor Store Security

Shopify store owners often underestimate how much misconfigured security settings silently drain revenue, rankings, and customer trust. PLS Security addresses the root causes — not just the symptoms.

Lost Organic Traffic

Blocked product and collection pages mean Google can't index your best content, directly reducing organic search traffic and sales.

Exposed Sensitive Data

Admin and checkout pages appearing in search results create security vulnerabilities that bad actors can exploit to access customer data.

Wasted Crawl Budget

Without proper crawl rules, Google wastes its crawl budget on irrelevant pages, leaving your most important product pages under-crawled and under-ranked.

Duplicate Content Penalties

Missing canonical tags and improper indexing rules cause duplicate content issues that trigger Google penalties and dilute your page authority.
